2. Incorporate regular exercise: In addition to following a calorie-controlled diet, it's important to incorporate regular physical activity to boost your metabolism and support weight loss. Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise per week, or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity exercise. This can be broken up into shorter sessions throughout the week, such as 30 minutes of exercise on five days. Choose activities that you enjoy, such as walking, cycling, or dancing, to make it easier to stick to your routine.

3. Choose weight loss-friendly foods: While calorie control is important, the types of foods you eat can also impact your weight loss success. Incorporate weight loss-friendly foods into your diet, such as fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains. These foods are generally low in calories and high in fiber, which can help you feel full and satisfied while also supporting weight loss.

4. Eat protein at every meal: Protein is an important nutrient for weight loss, as it helps to keep you feeling full and satisfied. Try to include protein at every meal and snack, such as eggs, chicken, fish, beans, or Greek yogurt.

5. Drink plenty of water: Staying hydrated is essential for weight loss, as it can help to flush toxins from your body and support healthy digestion.
